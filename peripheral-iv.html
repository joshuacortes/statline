Peripheral IV Services

Expert IV insertion and management for healthcare facilities, improving patient outcomes with safe and effective vascular access.

Peripheral IVs are **short-term vascular access devices** inserted into superficial veins, typically in the hands or arms. They are used for **fluids, medications, and blood draws**, providing essential access for patient care in hospitals, outpatient centers, and clinics.

Proper placement, maintenance, and monitoring are critical to **minimize complications** such as infiltration, phlebitis, or infection. Our licensed vascular access team follows strict protocols to ensure patient safety and regulatory compliance.

Understanding Peripheral IV Care

Peripheral IV therapy is not just about insertion; proper maintenance is key to patient safety. This includes:

  • Regular monitoring for infiltration, phlebitis, and infection
  • Proper dressing changes and aseptic technique
  • Safe administration of fluids and medications
  • Timely removal and replacement when clinically indicated

Our team educates facility staff on best practices and ensures **documentation and compliance** with all healthcare regulations. Partner with us to improve patient outcomes and streamline vascular access care.
